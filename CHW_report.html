Final takeaway

Applying the finalized OR-based definition flagged 33 providers. Manual validation confirmed 25 as CHW/CHR with 4 needing further review (total likely = 29).

Final definition (plain language)

Operational rule

Select providers/claims if any is true: provider name ends with “CHW”, OR specialty = 230, OR 98960–98962 and/or Z-codes in Dx1/Dx2.

Why taxonomy wasn’t primary

Evidence

We observed substantial 172V00000X volume even without 9896/Z signals, and also many 9896/Z records without 172V00000X, indicating taxonomy alone is inconsistent for primary identification.
